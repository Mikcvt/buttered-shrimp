Recipe for Preparing my all-time Favorite Dish, Buttered Shrimp.

Buttered shrimp is a mouthwatering seafood delight, showcasing plump and succulent shrimp bathed in a luxurious buttery sauce. The dish begins by saut√©ing fresh,
cleaned shrimp in a mixture of rich butter and aromatic garlic. The butter imparts a creamy texture while elevating the natural sweetness of the shrimp.
Complemented by a medley of herbs and spices such as parsley, paprika, and a hint of lemon juice, each bite bursts with savory and zesty flavors.
Served over rice, pasta, or crusty bread, buttered shrimp offers a quick, yet indulgent, culinary experience that is perfect for
both casual meals and special occasions.

    Ingridients

  • 2 lsb of Shrimp Cleaned
  • 2 tbps Chopped Parsley
  • 1/4 Cup of Butter
  • 1 Head of Garlic Crushed
  • i Cup of Lime Soda or Sprite
  • 1 Salt and Pepper

This video will show you the process of preparing it.

Step by Step Instructions.

  • 1st. Marinate the shrimp in lemon soda for about 10 minutes
  • 2nd. Melt the butter in a pan.
  • 3rd. Add the garlic. Cook in low heat until the color turns light brown
  • 4th. Put-in the shrimp. Adjust heat to high. Stir-fry until shrimp turns orange.
  • 5th. Season with ground black pepper, salt, and lemon juice. Stir.
  • 6th. Add parsley. Cook for 30 seconds.
  • 7th. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!
